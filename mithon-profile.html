Biography

I am Mithun Suttradhar. I am from Cumilla, Bangladesh. I passed SSC in the year 2015 with a GPA of 5.00 out of 5.00 and HSC in the year 2017 with a GPA of 4.33 out of 5.00. After that, I completed a Bachelor of Science in Statistics in 2023 from Victoria College, Cumilla, which is affiliated to the National University of Bangladesh, with a CGPA of 3.32 out of 4.00.

During my studies, I got acquainted with statistical analysis, predictive modeling, and data interpretation that laid the bedrock for my interest in Data Science. Along with academics, I acquired practical experience by participating in the government census survey led by the Bureau of Statistics under the Government of Bangladesh. This gave me hands-on experience with data collection and its analysis, showing how decisions can be made and societal problems solved through data.

Befor coming Japan, I was working as a Data Analysis Officer at Thengamara Mohila Sabuj Sangha (TMSS), which is a reputed NGO in Bangladesh. This exposure really nailed my interest in pursuing advanced studies in Data Science.

Regarding my family, I am single. My father runs a business in the medical sector and my mother is a housewife. I have an elder brother who runs a business and a younger sister who is a student.
